Links Policy
When sellers listing items on VBuy.in, sellers can include links that are solely intended to provide additional details about the items. That means links should only be used in circumstances when that information can't be included in the listing itself. These links include additional images, technical specifications, manufacturer warranties, and other information that can help buyers make an informed purchasing decision.
Other links that point to a third-party provider's Website are also allowed, only in case such as image hosting sites, payment processors or courier companies.
VBuy.in doesn't allow links to non-VBuy.in sites or content where other items are offered for sale. VBuy.in continually reviews links on the site and reserves the right to block any link considered unsafe for our members.
As a seller, please make sure that your listing follows links policy of VBuy.in. If it doesn't, it may be removed.
